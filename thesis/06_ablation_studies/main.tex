\chapter{Experimental Results \& Ablation Studies}
\label{cha:experimental_results}

This chapter presents the quantitative and qualitative results of the proposed GAN-based Sim-to-Real translation pipeline.
To validate the engineering choices described in previous chapters, we conducted a series of experiments and ablation studies.
We begin by defining the experimental setup and the hardware/software infrastructure used.
We then present a critical ablation study on data normalization, demonstrating why standard image processing techniques fail on high-dynamic-range radar data.
Finally, we evaluate the system's performance using the metrics defined in Chapter \ref{cha:methodology}.
